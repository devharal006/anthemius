How it works

Three steps. One conversation.

01
Speak
Describe the space - its function, area, number of occupants, and any constraints. No forms. No CAD. Just a conversation.
02

Anthemius produces multiple layout variations, each drawn to building code — zoning, circulation, and occupancy applied automatically.

Review
Browse variations, refine through conversation, and export the layouts you want to take forward. You stay in control at every step.
